This book store offers various categories of books including programming books, translations books, dictionaries and many more. It was established in the year 2001 by Narayana a college student. During his study days he faced alot of difficulties to find the books he needed and also he wanted to find a store which recommends the best books in a category. This led to his Idea for creating this book store which has professional book readers who can suggest the best books based on author, topic, category. The book store also supports online purchase of books and delivery. They contain almost every book and if they don't have the book you needed they will order the book and deliver it to you.
